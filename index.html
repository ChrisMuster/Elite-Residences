Welcome to Elite Residences

Elite Residences offer a luxury collection of serviced apartments to rent and property management services in central London providing space, freedom, and privacy alongside the luxury and service of a 5-star hotel. With a background in concierge services and hospitality, we understand our client’s needs.  As all our properties are in prime central London locations close to Hyde Park, we can offer our clients exceptional accommodation solutions.  From short-stay visits to explore the city to long-stay family relocation accommodation, Elite Residences offer a choice of apartments for leisure and corporate requirements.

With a growing portfolio of serviced apartments to choose from, we provide our clients with a choice of location, service, security and self-catering accommodation to suit their individual needs.  All apartments are modern and contemporary in design with fully-equipped kitchens, high-quality furnishings and bed linen, and the very latest in-room entertainment technology.  We strive hard to offer the best apartments, in the best London locations, providing home-from-home comfort and a perfect resting place to explore one of the most exciting, thrilling and diverse cities in the world.

Elite Residences is part of the Elite Lifestyle umbrella group, enabling us to offer a wide variety of Concierge services:

Housekeeping

We offer full housekeeping, from daily cleaning to laundry services. So you can get on and enjoy your stay without the worry of having to clean up. We’ve got it covered!

Cook-in Chef

We have access to some of the best chefs in London offering you a world-class culinary cook-in experience in the privacy of your own home. Whether you want a daily cook to serve you three meals a day or a specialist chef to prepare you a one-off meal, we can cater for all your needs.

Hospitality Services

We understand navigating London’s nightlife and restaurants can be a daunting task. Where to go, where to eat? Our personal customer service extends to our in-house reservation team, so let us take care of your every entertainment need. Want to book a last-minute table in a Michelin star restaurant? We’ve got that covered. Want a table in a private club? We can organise that for you. We endeavour to make your stay fun and unforgettable. For more information, contact our Reservations Team.

Mayfair Car Hire

Offering discreet, professional Rolls-Royce chauffeur hire and self-drive options. Choose from over 30 luxury high-end vehicles, from 4x4’s like the Mercedes G-Class G63 AMG and the Range Rover Autobiography, supercars like the Lamborghini Huracan and the Ferrari 458 Spider, to prestigious saloons like the Rolls-Royce Ghost and the Bentley Flying Spur. As well as chauffeur and self-drive options, we also offer airport transfers and close protection services.
