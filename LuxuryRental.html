Luxury Rental Accomodation

Elite Residences offer luxury serviced residences to rent in exclusive central London locations:

  • Mayfair
  • Kensington
  • Belgravia
  • Knightsbridge
  • Fitzrovia
  • Marylebone

With a growing portfolio plus concierge and hospitality experience, we offer independence, space and flexibility, alongside the luxury and service of a 5-star hotel. All our apartments are modern and contemporary in design and feature spacious living and dining areas, fully equipped kitchens, high quality furnishings and bed linen, and the very latest in-room entertainment.   We offer residences from spacious studio flats and one bedroom apartments with balconies, comfortable two, three or four-bedroom apartments ideal for families, to the ultimate in luxury penthouse suites and complete residences in the best London neighbourhoods. At Elite Residences, we understand that impeccable service and security are priorities for our guests.

We also offer a wide range of extra options including:

  • Airport transfers in luxury vehicles
  • Chauffeur and self-drive hire
  • Housekeeping
  • 5-star Chef cook-in service
  • Hospitality Services
  • Restaurant recommendations, theatre bookings and local area information

Our personal customer service extends to an in-house Reservations Team to manage availability and help guests with their individual requirements.  All members of our Reservations Team speak English and a second language to further assist with enquiries, and the office hours are:

8am-8pm weekdays

Saturdays 9am-4pm (UK)

For more information, please contact our Reservations Team.
